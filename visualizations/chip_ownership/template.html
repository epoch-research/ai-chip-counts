Methodology

All estimates use Monte Carlo simulation (5,000 samples) to propagate uncertainty.

Nvidia GPUs: Chip volumes are estimated from Nvidia's quarterly datacenter revenue, split by chip type using revenue share data, then divided by per-chip prices. Owner allocations are based on analyst estimates of each company's share of Nvidia datacenter revenue.

Google TPUs: TPU volumes are estimated from Broadcom's quarterly revenue attributed to Google's custom silicon program, split by TPU version using production mix estimates, then divided by per-chip manufacturing costs adjusted for Broadcom's margin.

H100e conversion: Units × (chip INT8 TOPS / 1,979), where 1,979 is the H100's 8-bit TOPS rating.
